Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Call Revive is an independently developed software application currently in research and development (R&D) phase. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used in connection with SMS communications sent on behalf of participating clinics.

Information We Collect

  • Patient phone numbers
  • SMS message content
  • Appointment-related details provided by clinics
  • Basic system logs (date, time, message status)

How Information Is Used

Information is used strictly for transactional healthcare communication purposes, including:

  • Missed call follow-up messages
  • Appointment reminders
  • Clinic-related responses to patient inquiries

No marketing or promotional messages are sent.

Data Sharing

Call Revive does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. SMS delivery is facilitated through secure third-party communication providers.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are implemented to protect data. As the system is currently in development, features and safeguards may evolve.

Opt-Out

Patients may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, no further SMS messages will be sent.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the system administrator through the associated clinic.
