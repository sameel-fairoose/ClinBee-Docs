SMS Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Clinbee is a software platform currently in research and development (R&D). It facilitates SMS communications on behalf of participating healthcare clinics.

Message Types

By providing your phone number to a participating clinic, you agree to receive transactional SMS messages including:

  • Missed call notifications
  • Appointment reminders
  • Clinic-related responses

No promotional or marketing messages are sent.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on patient interaction with the clinic.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message. After opting out, you will no longer receive messages.

Help

Reply HELP to receive assistance or contact your clinic directly.

Liability

Clinbee acts as a communication facilitator for clinics and is not responsible for medical decisions, diagnosis, or treatment.
